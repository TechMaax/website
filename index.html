Upcoming Family Events

Family Event

Thanksgiving Dinner

On July 10, 2024, the Johnson family gathered at Sunny Shores Beach for a delightful Family Beach Day. The event was filled with various fun activities, including beach volleyball and sandcastle building, providing entertainment for all ages. The highlight of the day was a delicious seafood cookout, where family members enjoyed freshly grilled fish, shrimp, and other beachside delicacies.

Upcoming Family Events

Annual Spring Picnic

On May 10, 2025, the Johnson family will gather at Riverside Park in Springfield for their Annual Spring Picnic. The day promises a relaxed atmosphere with fun games, a family photo session to capture precious memories, and an outdoor movie night under the stars. Families are encouraged to bring their picnic blankets and favorite snacks to share, making it a perfect day of bonding and enjoyment for all ages.

Upcoming Family Events

50th Wedding Anniversary Celebration for William and Emma

On June 12, 2023, the Johnson family gathered at the picturesque Green Valley Country Club to celebrate the 50th wedding anniversary of William and Emma. The evening began with a heartfelt vow renewal ceremony, where the couple reaffirmed their love and commitment to each other. The celebration continued with a lively reception featuring dancing, touching speeches from family and friends, and a nostalgic slideshow that showcased cherished moments from William and Emma's life together
